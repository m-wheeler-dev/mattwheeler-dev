Hover Colors!

If you like bright, randomized colors (or maybe you want to see if you might be colorblind) then this is for you! It's pretty self-explanatory, hover over the boxes and they become one of the random colors in the array. Once you leave that box, a timeout function runs and the color fades away. Like trying to write your name in the air with a torch, but way more colorful!
