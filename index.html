Hello, my name is Matthew Wheeler, I'm a Web Developer

About Me:

Don't worry, I'll skip the ancient history and go straight to the fun parts! I never thought I would get close to being a Web Developer, I was always working manual labor jobs and I enjoyed it so I wasn't concerned that I had no idea what HTML was. Fast forward to May, 2016... I was injured at work, and ended up with a permanent knee injury.

After 3 years of trying to recover, the insurance company finally agreed to a retraining program. With the budget and location, I decided to attend Edmonds College and pursue an Associate's Degree in Web Development. I ended up maintaining a 3.9GPA and being on the honor roll all 8 quarters. It turns out I really enjoy dev work, and I might even be good at it! I received my Associate's of Technical Arts Degree in Web Application and Cloud Development in August 2021.

Since graduating I've been networking, applying, learning, and making projects all the time to grow and better myself both personally and as a developer. In March of 2022 I was given the opportunity of a Software Engineer Internship at StudySoup. My work there revolved around updating their user experience while learning as much as I could. I learned more in those 10 weeks than I ever thought I would, and I'm very grateful to have that experience.
